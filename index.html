Fat Fingers - what’s in a name?

The specialty of Ergonomics guides us to design so that the small person can reach, and the large person can fit. Extending this to the constrained space of a mobile phone means striking a balance between the density and simplicity of information and of course making sure touch points are large enough for fat fingers.

Every fingerprint of space is valuable

In constrained screens there is no room for wasted space. We need to know what content engages customers and promote it ahead of content that doesn’t. Mobile-first design coupled with solid analytics is vital to monitor and maximise the power of every pixel.

Does your site look great on mobile?

The chances are, more eyes see your website on a small screen.

It makes good sense to make that as easy to use and engaging as your desktop site.

Successful websites are founded on good data

Audience analytics give you a clear idea of what works well on your website, and crucially what doesn't.
